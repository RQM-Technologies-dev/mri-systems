# Quaternionic Lift

Standard local complex MRI representation:

\[
z(r) = A(r)e^{i\phi(r)}
\]

Quaternionic lift used in this project:

\[
q(r) = A(r)[\cos\phi(r) + u(r)\sin\phi(r)]
\]

with unit imaginary axis \(u(r)\). The complex model is recovered when \(u(r)=i\), so quaternionic modeling is a strict generalization rather than a replacement of complex MRI.
