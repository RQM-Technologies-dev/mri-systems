# Claim 04: Motion/orientation robustness

## Claim

Quaternionic orientation-aware states may reduce ghosting/artifact sensitivity in
motion-perturbed controlled reconstructions.

## Evidence needed

- Synthetic motion/orientation perturbation generation
- Baseline and coherence-aware reconstructions
- Artifact-energy and ghosting proxy comparison

## Research target

10-20% ghosting/artifact reduction in controlled synthetic tests.

## Scope note

This is a hypothesis target and not a validated clinical or production claim.
