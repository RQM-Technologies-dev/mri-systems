# Claim 02: Artifact coherence mapping

## Claim

Coherence defect maps derived from quaternionic coil-state disagreement may
improve artifact-prone region localization versus magnitude-only indicators.

## Evidence needed

- Controlled artifact injection on synthetic data
- Region-of-interest artifact labels
- Detection metrics for baseline residual maps vs coherence defect maps

## Research target

10-25% improvement in artifact-region detection in controlled synthetic tests.

## Scope note

This is a research target and not an achieved performance statement.
